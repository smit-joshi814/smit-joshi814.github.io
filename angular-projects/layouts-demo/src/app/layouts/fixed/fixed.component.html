About Fixed Layouts

Fixed layouts have a set width and do not adapt to the screen size. They are useful for designs that require a consistent width, such as specific content layouts or traditional website designs.

  • Predictable Width: The layout width remains constant, providing a consistent viewing experience.
  • Alignment Control: Easier alignment of content as the width does not change.
  • Ideal for Desktop: Fixed layouts are best suited for desktop or larger screens where the width can be controlled precisely.

Examples of Fixed Layouts

Here are a few examples where fixed layouts are commonly used:

  • Corporate websites with specific branding and layout requirements.
  • Traditional blogs and news websites with a fixed content width.
  • Designs requiring precise control over the layout and alignment of elements.
